Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create an account?

    To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the instructions.

  • How do I enroll in a course?

    To enroll in a course, browse our course catalog and click on the "Enroll" button below to the course you want to take. Follow the instructions to complete the enrollment process.

  • How do I access my course materials?

    To access your course materials, log in to your account and go to your dashboard. From there, you can access all of your enrolled courses and the corresponding materials.
